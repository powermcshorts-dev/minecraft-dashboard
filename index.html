FAQ & Guide

Everything you need to know about the Decay SMP dashboard.

🗺️ What is this dashboard?
This is the Decay SMP live stats dashboard. It shows real-time player data, server health, and competitive rankings — all synced automatically from the Minecraft server every 30 seconds.
👥 What does the Players tab show?
The Players tab shows all registered SMP members as a grid of cards. Click any card to open a detailed stat panel showing their kills, deaths, K/D ratio, blocks mined, playtime, and more. Online players are highlighted.
🏆 What is the Leaderboard?
The Leaderboard ranks the players by their Overall (OVR) rating by default. You can switch the sort to Kills, Playtime, or Total Mined using the dropdown. The top 3 players get special styling highlights.
⚡ How are Player Ratings calculated?
Player ratings range from 1 to 99 and are calculated server-side based on your performance relative to other players:

• KIL (Kills): Scaled relative to the server's top killer.
• DTH (Survival): Scaled based on minimizing player deaths.
• DMD (Damage Dealt): Scaled relative to the highest damage dealt on the server.
• DMT (Damage Taken): Scaled based on minimizing damage taken.
• OVR (Overall): Calculated as the average of KIL, DTH, DMD, and DMT.
🏅 What are the Card Tiers?
Your player card design changes dynamically based on your OVR rating:

🟫 Bronze Card — OVR under 65
⬜ Silver Card — OVR 65 to 74
🟨 Gold Card — OVR 75 to 89
🌌 Master Card — OVR 90+ (features a special dark theme with custom neon highlights)
💻 What is TPS?
TPS stands for Ticks Per Second. Minecraft runs at 20 TPS normally. If TPS drops below 20, the server is struggling — things slow down, mobs lag, and the world feels sluggish. The TPS meter on the Server Health tab shows the live value.
⏱️ What is MSPT?
MSPT is Milliseconds Per Tick — how long it takes the server to process one game tick. The target is under 50ms (20 TPS). The color changes from green → yellow → orange → red as it gets worse. Above 50ms means the server is critically overloaded.
🔄 How often does data update?
Player stats are synced to the cloud every time a player joins or leaves, and automatically every 5 minutes while online. The dashboard refreshes every 30 seconds. You can also force a refresh using the ↺ button at the top right.
🎨 Why does my skin not show?
Skins are fetched from mc-heads.net using your SkinsRestorer skin name. If you set your skin using /skin set, it may take up to one relog to sync. If your skin still shows as Steve, ask an admin to check the server console for [WebStats Skin] logs.
⚡ What is the Live Events tab?
The Live Events tab is a permanent, historical log of all global events on the server. It tracks player events, combat deaths, and whitelist actions in real-time.
🌐 How does DDoS Detection work?
Our Sentinel Engine monitors incoming and outgoing bandwidth in real-time. The Network Usage meter on the Health tab uses a traffic-light system to warn staff of attacks:

🔵 Blue: Normal traffic
🟡 Yellow: High load warning
🔴 Red: Active DDoS / Network Saturation detected.
💬 How do I Whitelist via Discord?
To join the server, go to the #whitelist channel in our Discord and type your Minecraft username. Our bot will instantly whitelist you. Note: You can only whitelist ONE account per Discord user. If you need to change your account, please contact an admin.
